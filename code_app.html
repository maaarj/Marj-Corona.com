#Code App

The Problem

  • Too technical or complicated explanation frustrates students resulting in the inability to focus on one material or giving up on studying.
  • Students prefer video-based or mixed materials over text-based materials.
  • App notification or reminders are not effective in building habits of making time for studying.

Proposed Solution

  • Mixed learning materials that they can choose from that are short and digestible.
  • Quizzes at the end of each modules.
  • Showing them their study streak and progress per module.
  • Motivating or encouraging copy.
  • Certificate at the end of the course.

What have you learned from this project?

Research can never fail you. No matter how small the research is, it can always teach you something.

Although the user research here is very small because there are other requirements I have to accomplish, it still played a huge impact on our app.

We were able to freely generate as many ideas as we could then assess ideas against what I found on research and what is doable for us before we could implement. We were able to avoid the fear of not being able to implement the final design we passed to our Professor. We were also able to clearly define the tasks each of us needs to accomplish.
